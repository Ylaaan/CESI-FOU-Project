Hello, I'm Alex

I'm a passionate web developer and designer with over 5 years of experience creating digital experiences that make a difference. My journey in tech started with curiosity and has evolved into a career focused on building accessible, user-friendly applications.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing knowledge through writing and mentoring. I believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and improve people's lives.

My Background

My path into technology wasn't traditional. I started with a degree in psychology, which gave me a unique perspective on user behavior and human-computer interaction. This background has been invaluable in my work as a developer, helping me create more intuitive and empathetic user experiences.

After discovering my passion for coding through online courses and bootcamps, I made the transition into full-time development. Since then, I've worked with startups, agencies, and established companies, each experience teaching me something new about the craft of building software.
